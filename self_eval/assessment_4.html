Recepie: Cooking Pasta

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Water
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Pour water into a large pot with a lid. Put salt in the water. Close the lid and wait until the water boils.
  2. Once the water is boiling, add pasta into your water
  3. Wait for 12-15 minutes, depending on the pasta, and strain the water from the pot.
